## 📊 Overview

This indicator brings institutional trading concepts to your charts with full automation. It identifies **Break of Structure (BOS)**, **Change of Character (CHoCH)**, **Order Blocks**, **Fair Value Gaps**, and **Liquidity Sweeps**, then generates professional-grade entry signals with automatic SL/TP calculations.

Unlike basic SMC indicators, this system works like a professional trader: it **identifies setups forming**, **marks pending entry zones**, and **alerts you when to act** — not just when entries have already happened.

## 🎛️ How To Use

### **Step 1: Setup Configuration**
- Set your **HTF Timeframes** (default: Daily + 4H)
- Adjust **Risk:Reward** (default: 2.0)
- Enable/disable filters based on your strategy

### **Step 2: Watch for Setups**
1. **Status Table** shows current market state
2. **⏳ SETUP READY** label appears when conditions align
3. Wait for price to tap the **green/blue (long)** or **red/pink (short)** zone

### **Step 3: Entry Execution**
- **✅ LONG / 🔻 SHORT** label fires when entry triggers
- SL/TP automatically calculated and shown on label
- Set your alert or enter manually

### **Step 4: Trade Management**
- Monitor active trade in **Status Table**
- Receive **⚠ WARNING** if setup breaks down
- Exit at TP, SL, or on invalidation signal

## 💡 Trading Strategy

**For Long Entries:**
1. Daily & 4H bias must be bullish
2. Price in Discount zone (below midpoint)
3. BOS or CHoCH up confirmed
4. Price taps bullish OB or FVG
5. Optional: Liquidity sweep occurred
6. ✅ Entry fires with SL/TP

**For Short Entries:**
1. Daily & 4H bias must be bearish
2. Price in Premium zone (above midpoint)
3. BOS or CHoCH down confirmed
4. Price taps bearish OB or FVG
5. Optional: Liquidity sweep occurred
6. 🔻 Entry fires with SL/TP
